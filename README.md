# Culture Intelligence Platform

An open-source Business Intelligence platform designed for museums and cultural institutions.

The project integrates data collection, ETL, dimensional modeling, and interactive dashboards to support decision-making, heritage preservation, and cultural management.

## Vision

Transform cultural data into strategic information through an open, modular, and reusable Business Intelligence platform.

---

## Current Module

🟢 Museum Collections Observatory

A Business Intelligence solution focused on the management and analysis of museum collections, including preservation, digitization, temporal distribution, and collection profiling.
